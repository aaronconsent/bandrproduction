Frequently asked questions

What CNC machining services does B&R Productions offer?

CNC turning, CNC milling, manual machining, manual milling, and UNISIG-class deep-hole drilling. Seven CNC lathes, two CNC vertical machining centers, one deep-hole drilling machine, plus manual equipment. Full-service from prototype through production.

What alloys do you machine regularly?

Inconel 718 and 625, Super Duplex 2507, 17-4 PH, 4140, 4340, Monel K-500, titanium Ti-6Al-4V, 6061 and 7075 aerospace aluminum, 304/316 stainless, and tool steels. Oilfield exotic alloys weekly.

What industries do you primarily serve?

Oil & gas is our core industry — frac pump, wellhead (API 6A), downhole, and wireline components. We also serve aerospace, military & defense, and general industrial customers with the same tolerance discipline.

Where is B&R Productions located and what areas do you serve?

5909 Farm to Market Road 1374, New Waverly, TX 77358 — East Texas, between Huntsville and Conroe, about 60 miles north of Houston. We serve customers across Texas and the Gulf Coast, with regular work for Houston, Conroe, The Woodlands, Huntsville, Dallas, San Antonio, and Austin buyers.

Are you ISO 9001 certified?

Yes — ISO 9001:2015 certified. Full material traceability, heat and lot documentation, first-article inspection reports, and customer-specific quality plans available on request.

Can B&R handle emergency rig-down machining?

Yes. For existing customers on stocked alloys, we routinely turn rig-down parts same-day to next-day. New-customer emergency work depends on alloy availability and job scope — call us and we'll tell you honestly.

How do I request a quote?

Send a print, material spec, quantity, and quality documentation requirements to our quote form (linked in the header) or call (936) 291-7827. We'll respond with pricing and lead time within one business day for standard work, same day for rig-down.
