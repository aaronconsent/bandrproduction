Frequently asked questions

Do you pick up parts from Clear Lake?

For customers within a comfortable radius of New Waverly, yes — same-day or next-day pickup is realistic. For customers farther out, we ship via UPS/FedEx Ground and freight. Local pickup by prior arrangement — call (936) 291-7827.

How fast can you turn around parts for Clear Lake customers?

1–3 days for stocked-material prototypes (existing customer). 1–3 weeks for small production runs. Rig-down emergency: same-day to next-day when material is on the shelf.

What CNC machining services do you offer to Clear Lake manufacturers?

CNC turning (7 lathes), CNC milling (Fadal + YCM VMCs), UNISIG-class deep-hole drilling, and manual machining. Exotic alloys weekly — Inconel, Super Duplex 2507, 17-4 PH, titanium, Monel.

Do you take on Clear Lake rig-down or emergency machining?

Yes — emergency work is a specialty. Existing customers on stocked alloys can expect same-day to next-day turnaround. New customers get honest lead times based on material availability and job scope.

What tolerances do you hold on Clear Lake customer parts?

±0.0005" on critical dimensions is routine. Every critical feature gets first-article CMM verification and in-process gauging. Certified gauges and granite plates. Documented via first-article inspection reports.

How do I get a quote from B&R Productions for a Clear Lake project?

Send a print, material spec, quantity, and quality documentation requirements to our quote form or email sales@bandrproduction.com. Or call (936) 291-7827 during shop hours. Standard response within one business day; same-day for rig-down.
