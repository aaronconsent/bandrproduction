Frequently asked questions

What area does B&R Productions serve?

Our shop is at 5909 FM 1374, New Waverly, TX 77358 — between Huntsville and Conroe, about 60 miles north of Houston. We pick up and drop off across East Texas and the North Houston corridor, and ship parts anywhere in Texas and the Gulf Coast (Permian Basin, Eagle Ford, Haynesville) plus nationwide.

Do you offer local pickup and delivery?

For customers in Montgomery County, Walker County, and the North Houston corridor (Conroe, The Woodlands, Spring, Willis, Huntsville), yes — same-day and next-day pickup/delivery is realistic on emergency jobs. Farther out, we ship via UPS, FedEx Ground, and freight.

Can I visit the shop?

Yes — by appointment. Our facility is on FM 1374 with direct I-45 access via Highway 150 and Highway 105. Call (936) 291-7827 to schedule.

Do you serve the Permian Basin, Eagle Ford, and Haynesville?

Yes. We ship completed parts daily to oilfield operators and service companies across all three plays. Emergency rig-down work is routinely completed within 24–48 hours when the material is on our shelf.

Do you take work from outside Texas?

Yes. About 80% of our work is Texas-based, but we take on out-of-state jobs regularly — especially reverse-engineered oilfield sustainment parts, aerospace, and defense components. Nationwide ground and freight from New Waverly.
