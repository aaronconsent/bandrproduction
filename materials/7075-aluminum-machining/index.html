High-Strength Aerospace Aluminum · 7075

7075-T651 Aluminum CNC Machining

High-strength aluminum-zinc-magnesium-copper alloy — the aerospace primary structural workhorse. Nearly 2x the yield strength of 6061 with the same machinability, at the cost of weldability and corrosion resistance.

Frequently asked questions

Why 7075-T651 for plate work?

T651 stress-relieved plate has reduced residual stress after machining — critical for aerospace parts where stress-relief distortion would move features out of tolerance. Standard aerospace plate spec.

Do you machine large 7075 plates?

Yes — up to the YCM TV-188 B envelope (74" × 39" × 30"). Common for aerospace primary structural brackets.
