Downhole Tool Machining — Bodies, Mandrels, Subs, Crossovers

Downhole tools have to survive pressure, temperature, chemistry, and abuse. The parts have to be right the first time — because a re-do costs more than a job. That's the work B&R Productions is built for.

Materials we run weekly: Inconel 718 · Super Duplex 2507 · 4140 / 4340 pre-hard · 17-4 PH · Monel · Nitronic 50

What we machine

Downhole tool bodies. Mandrels. Subs and crossovers. Mud motor rotors and stators. Bit sub and shock sub components. Wireline running-tool parts. Fishing tool components. Rebuild kits from OEM or reverse-engineered prints.

Alloys and features we handle

Inconel 718, super duplex, 4140 and 4340 pre-hard, 17-4 PH, and Monel. API and custom threads — single-pointed in tough alloys. Long turned parts with the swing and bed length other job shops don't have.

Reverse engineering

Sending a used sample is fine. We inspect, model, prove out, and machine the replacement — with material traceability on the new part and first-article layout before we cut the production run.

Getting to us from the field

New Waverly is on FM1374 in Walker County, one hour north of Houston, with direct access to I-45 and Highway 105. We ship daily to service companies working the Permian, Eagle Ford, and Haynesville.

Frequently asked questions

Do you machine mud motor rotors and stators?

Yes.

Can you reverse-engineer a discontinued downhole tool from a sample?

Yes. This is a large share of our downhole work.

We handle long turned parts other job shops turn away. Send dimensions and we'll confirm the machine and lead time.
