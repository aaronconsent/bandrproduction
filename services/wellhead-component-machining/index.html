Wellhead Component Machining

Wellhead components have to seal, hold pressure, and stay dimensionally correct under load. B&R Productions machines them in 4130, 4140, 17-4 PH, and Inconel to API 6A tolerances — every job.

Materials we run weekly: 4130 / 4140 · 8630 · 17-4 PH · F22 · F91 · Inconel 718 · Inconel 625

What we machine

Wellhead bodies. Tubing and casing hangers. Bonnets. Adapter flanges. Master valve bodies. Christmas tree component parts. Studded connections. Flange faces with ring-groove finishes measured against the standard, not eyeballed.

Materials and traceability

4130, 4140, 8630, 17-4 PH, F22, F91 forgings, Inconel 718 and 625. Certified material with heat and lot documented on every job. Customer-specific ITPs and quality plans supported.

How we prove the part is right

First-article dimensional layout. CMM verification of critical dimensions on every job. Surface finish measured. If your quality manager wants a PPAP-style package, we build to it.
