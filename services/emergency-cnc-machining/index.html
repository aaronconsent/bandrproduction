Frequently asked questions

What counts as emergency turnaround?

Same-day to next-day for existing customers on stocked material. New customers with stocked-alloy work: typically 2–5 days including verification. Rush pricing applies.

Is emergency work more expensive?

Rush pricing reflects overtime, weekend work, and priority sequencing. Typically 20–50% premium depending on schedule impact. Discussed upfront.
