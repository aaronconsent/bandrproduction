Frac Pump Machining in Texas

When a fluid end cracks or a plunger scores mid-stage, the clock starts. B&R Productions machines frac pump components in the exotic alloys they demand — and we prioritize the jobs that are keeping a fleet down.

Materials we run weekly: Inconel 718 · Inconel 625 · Hastelloy C-276 · 17-4 PH · Super Duplex 2507 · Duplex 2205 · Nitronic 50/60 · 4340 pre-hard · Monel

What we machine

Fluid ends and packing bores. Plungers in 17-4 PH, Nitronic 50, and Inconel-clad. Suction and discharge valve bodies. Valve seats. Valve covers and retainers. Discharge cross bodies. Stuffing box components. Whether you send a print or a worn sample, we build it.

Alloys we run every week

Inconel 718 and 625, Hastelloy C-276, 17-4 PH, Super Duplex 2507, Duplex 2205, Nitronic 50/60, 4340 pre-hard, and Monel. Sharp tooling and proven feeds and speeds for each — your part is not the one we're experimenting on.
