Frac Pump Machining in Texas

When a fluid end cracks or a plunger scores mid-stage, the clock starts. B&R Productions machines frac pump components in the exotic alloys they demand — and we prioritize the jobs that are keeping a fleet down.

Materials we run weekly: Inconel 718 · Inconel 625 · Hastelloy C-276 · 17-4 PH · Super Duplex 2507 · Duplex 2205 · Nitronic 50/60 · 4340 pre-hard · Monel

What we machine

Fluid ends and packing bores. Plungers in 17-4 PH, Nitronic 50, and Inconel-clad. Suction and discharge valve bodies. Valve seats. Valve covers and retainers. Discharge cross bodies. Stuffing box components. Whether you send a print or a worn sample, we build it.

Alloys we run every week

Inconel 718 and 625, Hastelloy C-276, 17-4 PH, Super Duplex 2507, Duplex 2205, Nitronic 50/60, 4340 pre-hard, and Monel. Sharp tooling and proven feeds and speeds for each — your part is not the one we're experimenting on.

Emergency turnaround

When your equipment is offline and burning day-rate, the fastest shop wins. Same-day and next-day are realistic when the material is on the shelf. Call (936) 291-7827 and we'll tell you honestly what we can commit to.

How buyers work with us

Send a print (PDF, STEP, IGES, DWG) or drop off a sample. We reverse-engineer discontinued OEM parts routinely — including for operators keeping older pump models in service after the OEM stopped supporting them. NDAs on request.

Frequently asked questions

How fast can you turn around emergency frac pump work?

Same-week is realistic for most jobs; same-day and next-day when the material is on the shelf. Call (936) 291-7827 and we'll give you a straight answer.

Can you reverse-engineer discontinued OEM frac pump parts?

Yes. Send us a sample and we build the replacement. This is a big share of our O&G work — operators keeping older pump models in service after the OEM stopped supporting them.

What tolerances do you hold on plungers and valve components?

±0.0005" routine on critical features, better on request. Every critical dimension gets first-article layout and CMM verification.

Do you machine complete fluid end blocks?

Yes — from stress-relieved forgings, in the alloy you specify.
