How our capabilities map to your part

Turned parts (shafts, plungers, mandrels, wellhead spools, downhole tool bodies) go to our CNC turning centers. Small-envelope precision on the Hi-Eco pair, large heavy work on the Mega and Samsung SL-65A. Prismatic parts (aerospace brackets, wellhead bodies, complex 3D geometry) go to CNC milling on the Fadal or YCM. Deep central bores (long L/D oilfield tool bodies, hydraulic cylinders) go to UNISIG-class deep-hole drilling. Manual work handles one-offs, fixtures, and prototype-scale jobs.

Frequently asked questions

What CNC machining services does B&R Productions offer?

CNC turning, CNC milling, manual machining, and manual milling. Seven CNC lathes, two CNC mills, one UNISIG-class deep-hole drilling machine, plus manual lathes and mills for one-off precision work. Full-service from prototype through production.

What alloys do you machine regularly?

Inconel 718 and 625, Super Duplex 2507, 17-4 PH, 4140, 4340, Monel K-500, titanium Ti-6Al-4V, 6061 and 7075 aerospace aluminum, and 304/316 stainless. Oilfield exotic alloys weekly.

What industries do you serve?

Primarily oil & gas (frac pump, wellhead, downhole, wireline), with substantial work in aerospace, military & defense, and general industrial. Full alloy fluency across all four.

Where is B&R Productions located?

5909 Farm to Market Road 1374, New Waverly, TX 77358 — East Texas, between Huntsville and Conroe, about 60 miles north of Houston. Serving customers across Texas and the Gulf Coast.

What quality documentation do you provide?

Full material traceability, heat + lot documentation, Mill Test Reports (MTRs), first-article inspection reports, certificates of conformance, and customer-specific quality plans available on request.
