Frequently asked questions

Do you have AS9100 certification?

Contact us for current certification status and to discuss customer-specific quality plans. We work to program-specific quality requirements and provide full material traceability, MTRs, first-article inspection reports, and PPAP-style documentation on every aerospace job.

Can you machine titanium and Inconel for aerospace applications?

Yes — both are weekly work. Titanium Ti-6Al-4V for structural brackets and hardware. Inconel 718 (aged) for high-temperature engine components. Sharp coated carbide, controlled feeds, high-pressure through-tool coolant on the Hi-Tech and Mega lathes.

What tolerances do you hold on aerospace parts?

±0.0005" routine on critical dimensions. True-position, profile, and runout tolerances per drawing. CMM verification standard on first article and periodic in-process.

Do you handle DFARS-compliant material sourcing?

Yes. DFARS-compliant specialty metals sourcing available for aerospace and defense programs. Material Test Reports (MTRs) traced back to qualified US mills. Discuss specific program requirements at quote time.

What aerospace primes and programs have you supported?

We've supported multiple aerospace tier-1 and tier-2 suppliers on structural, engine-adjacent, and UAV programs since 1994. Confidentiality is standard — send an NDA if program details need to be discussed formally.

Do you serve the Dallas / DFW aerospace corridor?

Yes — Dallas and DFW aerospace contractors are a significant portion of our aerospace work. Regular ground shipping to DFW. In-person meetings by appointment; New Waverly to Dallas is about 3.5 hours.

Have a aerospace part? Send the print — we'll quote it.
