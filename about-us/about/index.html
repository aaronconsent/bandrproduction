About B&R Productions: Precision CNC Machining in Texas Since 1994

Since 1994, B&R Productions has been a trusted Texas CNC machine shop, delivering precision machining services for oil & gas, aerospace, defense, and industrial manufacturing. What began as a small machine shop has grown into a full-service operation with over 25 CNC machines, manual machining capabilities, and a reputation for accuracy, reliability, and speed.

Our team combines decades of hands-on experience with advanced machining technology to produce mission-critical components in stainless, titanium, Inconel, Hastelloy, and other alloys.

Our Approach to Precision Machining

At B&R Productions, our approach is built on reliability, transparency, and measurable results.
