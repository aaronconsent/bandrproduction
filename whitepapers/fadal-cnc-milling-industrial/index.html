The machine

CNC milling is the versatile leg of the shop — where valve bodies, wellhead adapters, connectors, brackets, and any part that isn't a simple turn gets made. The Fadal is reliable, well-understood, and rigid enough for the harder alloys. Fine for prototypes and repeat production runs alike.

Alloys we run for Industrial & General Manufacturing

Full range — stainless (304, 316, 17-4 PH), tool steels, 4140/4340, brass, bronze, aluminum, cast iron, and specialty alloys per customer requirement
