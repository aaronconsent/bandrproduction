Precision gundrilling and BTA drilling for long, straight, tight-tolerance holes. This paper covers the machine's capabilities, the oil & gas parts we produce with it, the alloys we run, and the documentation package.

The machine

Deep, straight, concentric holes are one of the hardest features to produce on any CNC platform without a purpose-built machine. Most job shops don't have one. That means downhole tool bodies, valve stems with long bores, hydraulic cylinders, and any part that needs a straight bore longer than a few diameters routinely gets outsourced — or worse, made poorly on an inadequate setup. UNISIG deep-hole drilling is a genuine capability moat.
